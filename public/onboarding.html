Welcome to CrateMatch!

Let's get you set up to convert Spotify playlists into Serato crates in just a few clicks.

First, we need your Serato database

1

Find your Serato database

Open your _Serato_ folder and locate the "database V2" file. It's usually found here:

macOS: ~/Music/_Serato_/database V2
2

Upload your database

Click the upload area below and select your "database V2" file. You can upload again in the settings page when you update your library later on.

Upload your Serato database file
Drag and drop your "database V2" file here, or click to browse
